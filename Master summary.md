## Things this entire reconciliation does *not* cover

Bears repeating from the original cross-check, because it's the most important framing point: even after every adjustment above, this is an **expense-only** reconciliation. Indian matrimonial settlements typically also consider:

- **Asset distribution** — properties, vehicles, jewellery, mutual funds, equity, EPF, FDs (none of these are in her file).
- **Income disparity** — Form 16s, ITRs. Who earned the household income matters legally regardless of who routed the payments.
- **Future maintenance / child support** — Oorvi's custody, maintenance, education funding.
- **Standard of living and length of marriage**.

A ₹0 expense gap does not mean ₹0 settlement. A ₹35L expense gap also doesn't mean ₹35L settlement. **These numbers are inputs to your lawyer's view — not the settlement number itself.**
